How It Works

1. Register

Create your account with your school email and fill in your academic profile (year group, subjects, availability).

2. Create Requests

Post requests to offer tutoring or to seek help in specific subjects and timeslots that fit your schedule.

3. Get Matched

An admin runs the matching algorithm — you'll see your matched partner on the dashboard and can contact them directly.
