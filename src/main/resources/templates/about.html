About the Student Portal

Our Mission

The Bromsgrove School Student Portal is designed to facilitate peer-to-peer tutoring and academic support among our student community. We believe that students can learn effectively from each other while building valuable teaching and communication skills.

How It Works

1. Complete Your Profile

Set up your academic profile including year group, subjects, and availability.

2. Create Requests

Post requests to offer tutoring or seek help in specific subjects and time slots.

3. Get Matched

Our system will match you with suitable tutoring partners based on your needs and availability.

4. Start Learning

Begin your tutoring sessions and support each other's academic journey.

Guidelines

  • All tutoring sessions should be conducted in appropriate school spaces or designated areas
  • Maintain professionalism and respect in all interactions
  • Report any issues or concerns to the Academic Support Office
  • Sessions should focus on understanding concepts, not completing assignments for others
  • Both tutors and tutees are expected to be punctual and prepared
